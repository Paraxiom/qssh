## Why This Model

Paraxiom's mission is to build post-quantum infrastructure that is verifiable, auditable, and trustworthy. We publish our formal proofs, our benchmarks, and our binaries because trust requires transparency.

We do not publish source code openly because we are a small team building critical infrastructure without external funding. Open-sourcing the implementation would allow well-funded actors to rebrand and deploy our work without contributing back. We choose collaboration over extraction.

If you share our values — building secure infrastructure through cooperation rather than competition — we want to work with you. Ask for access. We say yes far more often than no.
